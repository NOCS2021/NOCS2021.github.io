Format

Electronic paper submission requires a full paper, up to 8 double-column ACM ("sigconf") format pages, including figures and references (ACM Proceedings Template). An abstract of up to 250 words must be included. Papers should be formatted as close as possible to the final format. The acceptable format is PDF only. Any other file format and manuscripts received in hard-copy form will not be processed.

Important

NOCS 2021 utilizes a blind review process. When preparing your manuscript, do not include any information which could reveal the identity of the authors. The title section of your manuscript should not contain any author, affiliation status, email addresses, or contact information. In the body of the submission you should reference any of your prior work only if absolutely necessary and as a third party reference, e.g., "we improve the work reported in [x] by …". We need your cooperation in our effort to maintain a fair blind review process.
