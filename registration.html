Registration

All registrations are made through the ESWEEK registration webpage. The registration provides full access to all the ESWEEK events (CASES, CODES+ISSS, EMSOFT, and NOCS). All NOCS registrants should select NOCS on the registration page as the primary event for which they are registering.

Authors presenting papers should register as "Conference/Symposium Presenter".
